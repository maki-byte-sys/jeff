Carbonara

Creamy Filipino-style Carbonara is a rich pasta dish made with bacon, cream, and parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Bacon or ham
  • Garlic
  • Heavy cream
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Cooking Method

Cook pasta, sauté bacon and garlic, mix with cream and egg mixture, then toss everything together with pasta until creamy.
