Pancit Canton

Stir-fried Filipino noodles with vegetables, meat, and soy-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pancit canton noodles
  • Chicken or pork
  • Carrots & cabbage
  • Garlic & onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil

Cooking Method

Sauté meat and vegetables, add noodles and sauce, stir-fry until cooked and flavorful.

Reference: Kawaling Pinoy – Pancit Canton
