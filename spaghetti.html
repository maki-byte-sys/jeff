Filipino Spaghetti

Sweet-style Filipino spaghetti, typically served with hotdogs and ground meat.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Hotdogs
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Garlic & onions

Cooking Method

Cook pasta, sauté garlic, onions, beef and hotdogs, add tomato sauce, simmer, then mix with pasta and top with cheese.

Reference: Panlasang Pinoy – Filipino Spaghetti
